How and why

My favorite part of the game is the early-game. It reminds me of how I played the game in my childhood. Mismatched iron gear, simple builds, fearing the night. I want to change the game in subtle ways to bring back that feeling.

Modifications

  • Fabulously Optimized — optimization modpack (Fabric/Quilt)
  • webby — datapack I made that makes spiders web you you
  • worldgen modifications
    • Terralith — better overworld terrain
    • Structory — better overworld structures
    • Incendium — better nether terrain
    • Nullscape — better end terrain
  • (planned) nerf enchanting tables, buff exploration loot
  • (planned) remove villager trades that give you emeralds

Playstyle

  • The goal is not to get the best gear, or have the biggest farms. The goal is to build a beautiful world.
  • basic, almost childlike building style
  • leave the stump when cutting trees
  • build little things everywhere you go.
    • boat docks
    • small houses
    • resource caches
    • ladders, stairs, bridges, paths
    • other decoration
  • don't desecrate the world — clean up after yourself
    • fill in creeper holes
    • build with the geography, not against it
  • moody brightness looks better.
